# Retro: learn from what happened

Scope first: **personal** (a task/session) or **team** (an iteration/sprint). Adjust depth accordingly.

## Steps
1. **Timeline.** Briefly reconstruct what was done and what happened (from the work, commits, or the session).
2. **What went well.** Concrete wins worth repeating.
3. **What went wrong.** Problems, friction, surprises — facts, not blame.
4. **Root causes.** For the top issues, why did they happen? (not just symptoms)
5. **Action items.** Specific, owned (team), and time-bound where possible. Few and real beats many and vague.

## Feed-back loop (this skill's edge)
If a recurring AI/process issue surfaces, propose a concrete change:
- a new/edited rule in `rules/`, a new/edited skill in `skills/`, or a doc update.
Suggest it explicitly — don't apply unless asked.

## Output
Short structured summary: Went well / Went wrong / Root causes / Action items (+ any proposed rule/skill/doc change).
